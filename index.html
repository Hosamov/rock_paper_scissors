Rock Paper Scissors

Game Setup

How many cards should be in your deck?


18 cards

How the game works:

Start the game initially by shuffling the deck of cards.

Two cards are added to the play area facing down.

Note: AI's is the top card; Player's is the bottom card.

Player must click/tap the bottom card to flip the cards over.

The player with the higher card takes both cards (both cards are automatically given to the winner of the round)

Playing continues until one player wins the "battle".


A note on tie rounds

When two drawn cards match, the cards will be automatically added to the tie pot.

The tie pot will be awarded to the next winning player.

If a tie occurs when a player has 1 card remaining, the player with the most cards is awarded the tie pot and hence that player wins the battle.
